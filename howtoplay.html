Command Description
/help Displays a list of available commands
/rtp Teleport to a random location
/nation The main nation command. Nations are groups of players who have the ability to create a unified claim
/claim Claim your land to protect your base! You are able to claim in chunks only next to your existing claims.
/unclaim Revoke your claim!
/claiminfo Check the information of your claim!
/claimtrust Add, remove, or list players you trust so that they can build in your personal claim!
/display Visualize your claim!
/shop Upgrade your claim chunks and more!
