E.N.D [HISTORICAL NATION]

Summary: The END is a nation, settled near the center of Hesperia, that has been a symbol of freedom since the beginning of Jeracraft. Being a nation built upon the 1st discovered End portal in Jeracraft, the END nation is an elitist empire, well-known for its dominance in holding the most massive farms and building projects. As one of the 8 founding members of the Council of Nations, the END is a place for any dedicated player to be Empowered, Noble, & Dynamic on their ambitions towards the server.

Origin Story:

Towards the beginning of the pre-updaterian era, a lone explorer by the name of MintyFenix came across the 1st end portal during her travels. Fascinated by the grand nature of her discovery, Minty decided to claim the end portal for herself and build an entire nation around it. She would soon learn that the path towards this goal would not be easy, as conflict arose over the ownership of the end portal. Players, nations, and even the gods argued against Minty's claim of the portal, but she stood strong. And after settling on a blood pact with the gods, that ensured her claim so long as she provided free access to the portal, the END nation was born.
