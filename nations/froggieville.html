Kingdom of Froggies

Summary: The Kingdom of Froggies (also known as Froggieville) is one of the largest nations in Jeracraft, settled on the north side of Saxumval. Boasting one of the most massive & beautiful cities to ever be constructed, an interactive community, and one of the richest centers of lore, it is no wonder why they are one of the 8 founders of the Council of Nations. For any skilled builder, worker, or roleplayer in search of a vibrant city, the Kingdom of Froggies is the place for you.

Origin Story:

Government/Structure: The Kingdom of Froggies has one of the most unique structures of any nation within Jeracraft as the nation seems to have two organization systems. One for national responsibilities, and another for roleplay. Making the Kingdom of Froggies the only nation to put such emphasis in RP.

The general officers of the Kingdom of Froggies and trusted officials who answer right below the head figures of the nation. Each head leader within the Kingdom of Froggies has a right to be entitled to a certain # of chairmen who answer directly to their corresponding leader and are in charge of directing the orders & projects each head commands. And beneath all of the above lie the regular citizens of Froggieville who are assigned roles based on their contributions towards the nation, which living district they reside in, and also their RP alignment.
