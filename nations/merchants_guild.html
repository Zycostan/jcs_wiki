Merchant's Guild

Summary: The Merchant_s Guild, a nation settled in the middle of Terrvumina, is an organization whose name is stapled across Jeracraft as the world’s biggest economy center. Being not only one of the 8 founding members of the Council of Nations but also one of the wealthiest factions on the server, the Merchant’s Guild is the to-go place for any traveler seeking to buy some wares.

Origin Story:

Long ago during the beginning era of Jeracraft, there existed a small gathering of 5 merchants (Eden, Nitro_345, Sli4eva, CStefan, & Sivarius) who banded together towards a dream. A dream to create a vast wealthy trade empire that dominates the market. Thus, the Merchant’s Guild was born and thanks to their efforts, its influence across Jeracraft had grown exponentially, with their name cemented as the biggest trading company in all of Jeracraft
