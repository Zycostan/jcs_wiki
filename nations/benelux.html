Benelux [HISTORICAL NATION]

Summary: The city of Benelux is a nation settled amidst the lands of South Pandora focused on one goal. To build the biggest and most beautiful town that Jeracraft has ever seen. Serving as one of the 8 founders of the Council of nations and a kingdom that has existed since the origination of Jeracraft, the city of Benelux shall always be known as of the prominent projects on the server.

Origin Story:

It is said that long ago before even the great Frostwall was created, there once existed a great city by the name of Benelux. However, as much as the rest of humanity beyond the wall, the city of Benelux was destroyed leaving its legacy left in legends.
